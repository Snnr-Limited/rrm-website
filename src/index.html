How It Works

Compliance in Four Simple Steps

Getting started with RRM takes minutes. Staying compliant takes almost no effort at all.

01

Sign Up & Set Up

Create your restaurant profile, configure your Food Control Templates, and invite your team — all in under 10 minutes.

02

Fill Digital Forms

Staff complete compliance forms on their phones using touch, voice, or camera input. No clipboards, no handwriting.

03

IoT & AI Do the Rest

Connected sensors auto-log temperature and acidity readings. AI captures deliveries and processes paper forms via camera — your records fill themselves.

04

Export for Auditors

When an inspection is due, export a complete, timestamped compliance report with one tap and share it instantly with your auditor.
